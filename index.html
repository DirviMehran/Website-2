About Me

I am a dynamic academic and researcher with expertise in Logistics, Supply Chain Management, and Engineering Management. With academic appointments spanning Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, and the UK, my teaching and research integrate sustainability, optimization, artificial intelligence, and data analytics to tackle complex supply chain challenges.
